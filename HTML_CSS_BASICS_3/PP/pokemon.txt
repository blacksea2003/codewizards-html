Pokemons

Pokemons are powerful creatures, somewhat like animals but with special super powers.

Pikachu

Pikachu is one of the best pokemons, it can shed lightning to electrocute others. Apart from being powerful it also looks very cute specially when it says "pika pika".

Eevee

Eevee is a mammalian, quadruped creature with primarily brown fur. The tip of its bushy tail and its large furry collar are cream-colored. It has short, slender legs with three small toes and a pink paw pad on each foot.

Charizard

Charizard is a draconic, bipedal Pokémon. It is primarily orange with a cream underside from the chest to the tip of its tail, which burns with a sizable flame. Charizard has a long neck, small blue eyes, raised nostrils, and two blunt horns protruding from the back of its rectangular head.
